Programs of Study

The department offers the undergraduate major of Computer Science. We also offer two master degree programs: Computer Science, and Computer Information Systems and Cyber Security.


Scholarships

There are several undergraduate scholarships and graduate assistantships available to students majoring in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems and Cyber Security.


Student Clubs and Organizations


We have three active student clubs – Mathematics Club, Computer Science Club, and Engineering Club.

Faculty and Staff

The Computer Science faculty and staff are here to help you be successful. We are reachable by phone or email and you are welcome to stop by us.


Academic Advising

Our advisors are here to help. Proper advising will serve to make your time here enjoyable. The combination Computer Science major with minors such as Mathematics makes students very attractive to employers.


Research Interns and Seminars


Our faculty conduct various types of research in the areas of computer science and information systems. Students have opportunities to participate in research projects.
